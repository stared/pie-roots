PIE roots

How single Proto-Indo-European words fan out into the modern vocabulary — one root per page, every chain verified against the sources. A companion to the tree of tree.

*h₂stḗr — the star of ‘star’

star, stellar, asterisk, disaster, étoile — a scrollytelling explorable

*weyd- means to see, to know

vision, view, evidence, wit, wise, history, idea — and Hades, the Unseen

*weyd- means magic

wizard, clairvoyant, déjà vu, eidolon — and Polish wiedźma, wieszcz, widmo

*bʰeh₂- ‘to speak’

fate, fairy, prophet, euphemism, anthem, ban, bandit, contraband

*sed- ‘to sit’

sit, settle, seat, siege, size, chair, banshee, Upanishad

*kʷel- ‘to turn’

wheel, cycle, chakra, culture, colony, pole, palindrome, collar
