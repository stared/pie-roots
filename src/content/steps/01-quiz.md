Which of these words has a star hidden inside: **disaster**, **starve**, **étoile**, **strategy**, **Esther**, **stark**?

The letters mislead in both directions. *Disaster* and *étoile* carry a star no one sees; *Esther* — maybe. *Starve*, *strategy*, and *stark* flash the letters and never had one. Spelling is not kinship. Descent is — and this chart draws only descent: every word here grew, step by attested step, from one Proto-Indo-European word for star.
