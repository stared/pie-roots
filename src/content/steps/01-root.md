title: The star in the centre
focus: [h2ster]
---

At the centre of this chart sits **\*h₂stḗr** — the word for *star* in Proto-Indo-European, spoken some six thousand years ago and never written down. We reconstruct it from its descendants. It probably meant *the glowing one*, from **\*h₂eh₁s-** *to burn* — though even that reading is uncertain.
