title: The glower
focus: [h2ster]
---

At the center: **\*h₂stḗr**, the word linguists reconstruct for the language spoken on the steppe some six thousand years ago. Wiktionary, following Ringe, reads it as *\*h₂e(h₁)s-* ‘to burn, glow’ plus the agent suffix *-tḗr* — literally **“the one that glows.”** Even that is hedged. Below the root, reconstruction goes opaque — you cannot see into a star’s core.
