title: From steorra to star
focus: [pgmc-sterno]
---

In Germanic the word became **\*sternǭ**, then Old English **steorra**, then simply **star**. German **Stern** and Old Norse **stjarna** walk beside it — and the Norse word sailed to England on its own, surviving as Scots **starn**. Of all the words here, **star** changed the least; the surprises live on the other arms.
