The same word, reshaped by other mouths over millennia: Welsh **seren**, Armenian **աստղ [astł]**, Tocharian **ścirye**, Hittite **ḫašterz** — each a regular descendant of **\*h₂stḗr**. Sanskrit **तारा [tā́rā]** dropped its *s-* within recorded history and names the goddess **Tārā** — though the familiar given name Tara is Irish, *a high place*, with no star in it.
