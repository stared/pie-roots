---
key: disaster
title: Is there a star in disaster?
focus: [disaster, asterisk, astrolabe]
---

**Disaster** came through Italian *disastro*, from Greek *dus- + astḗr*: an *ill-starred* event, a calamity written in the sky. The **asterisk** is *asterískos*, the *little star* Greek scribes drew in the margin. And the **astrolabe** is the *star-taker* — an instrument named for what it does.
