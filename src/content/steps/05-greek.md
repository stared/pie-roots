key: greek
title: The hidden stars of Greek
focus: [disaster, asterisk, astrolabe]
---

**Disaster** is *dis- + astro* — an event that happened under a bad star; the astrology is fossilized in the spelling. The **astrolabe** is the ‘star-**taker**’ (*ἄστρον* + *λαμβάνειν*): the instrument’s name says what it does. The **asterisk** is the ‘little star’ — and its Latin twin *asteriscus*, unchanged, names a small bone of the inner ear.
