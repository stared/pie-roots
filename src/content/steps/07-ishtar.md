---
key: ishtar
title: Does Ishtar come from star?
focus: [hit-hasterz, h2ster, esther]
---

The family’s oldest written star is Hittite **ḫašterz**, pressed into clay 3,500 years ago. Its initial *ḫ-* keeps a consonant that a borrowing of *Ishtar* could not supply — so the word is inherited, not the goddess’s. Some now argue the loan ran the other way: that Semitic took Ishtar’s very name from Indo-European. And **Esther**? Persian for *star* — or Ishtar herself; the two readings blur into one.
