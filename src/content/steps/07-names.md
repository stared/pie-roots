**Esther**: from Persian *setāre* ‘star’ — or from **Ishtar**? The two theories are not independent; even on the Persian reading, the name likely alludes to the goddess. **Tara** is a double trap: the Buddhist deity’s name carries the Sanskrit star, but the common given name is Irish — *Teamhair*, ‘high place’, the Hill of Tara. Same letters, different stories.
