title: The Ishtar question
focus: [hit-hasterz, h2ster, esther]
---

The family’s oldest written form is cuneiform: Hittite **ḫašterz**. Its initial *ḫ-* preserves a laryngeal consonant that a borrowing of *Ishtar* could not explain — the standard argument that Indo-European **inherited** its star word. The live question now runs the other way: Wilson-Wright (2015) argues that *Semitic* borrowed *ʕAṯtar-* — Ishtar’s own name — from Indo-European. Unresolved; the popular meme has the arrow backwards either way.
