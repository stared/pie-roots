key: sidus
title: desire, consider — no relation
focus: []
---

Latin had a second star word, **sīdus** *constellation*, unrelated to *stēlla*. It hides in **consider**, perhaps *to examine the stars*, and in **desire**, perhaps *to await what the stars will bring* — so English carries hidden stars from two separate roots. Balto-Slavic, meanwhile, watched the same sky with neither: Polish **gwiazda** and Russian **звезда [zvezdá]** grow from a root of their own.
