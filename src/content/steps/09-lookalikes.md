And the words that flash *st–r* at you? **Starve** and **stark** come from **\*ster-** *stiff* — to starve was simply *to die*, as German **sterben** still does. **Street**, **stratum** and **strategy** come from **\*sterh₃-** *to spread*. **Sterile** is *barren*, **sitar** is Persian *three-string*, and **Zoroaster** hides a camel (Avestan *uštra*), not a star. The one that looks most like a fake — **asteroid**, coined, scientific, suspiciously tidy — is the genuine article.
