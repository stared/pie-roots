focus: []
---

**Starve** (‘become stiff’ — cf. German *sterben*), **stark**, **stare**, **stern**, **sterile** (‘barren’), **street** and **strategy** (‘spread out’), **sitar** (‘three-string’), **Zoroaster**, even the *-aster* of **poetaster** — five different roots and suffixes, and not one star among them. The reverse trap closes the loop: **asteroid** looks like the fake — coined, scientific, suspiciously tidy — and is the genuine article.
