Every link in this chart is a claim by fallible scholars, not a fact about prehistory. Dashed lines mark links disputed in print; solid ones mean only that no published disagreement was found. Click any word for its sources: Wiktionary and etymonline throughout, cross-checked against Watkins’ *American Heritage Dictionary of Indo-European Roots*, Pokorny, and the Leiden etymological dictionaries (Beekes, Kroonen, de Vaan, Kloekhorst, Matasović, Martirosyan, Mayrhofer).
