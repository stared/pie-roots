Every link in this chart is a claim by fallible scholars, not a fact about prehistory — *solid* here means only “no published disagreement found.” Dashed lines mark links where scholars disagree in print. Click any word for its sources: Wiktionary and etymonline throughout, cross-checked against Watkins’ *American Heritage Dictionary of Indo-European Roots*, Pokorny, and the Leiden etymological dictionaries (Beekes, Kroonen, de Vaan, Kloekhorst, Matasović, Martirosyan, Mayrhofer).
